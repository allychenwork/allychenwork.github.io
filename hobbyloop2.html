HobbyLoop

UI/UX Design • Mobile App Concept • 2025

Overview

HobbyLoop is a mobile app designed to help users discover, join, and sustain hobbies through structured social engagement.

Problem

Many users struggle to maintain hobbies due to lack of accountability, structure, and community engagement.

Process

Research

User interviews and affinity mapping revealed patterns in motivation loss.

Ideation

Explored gamification and accountability loops to improve retention.

Solution

A structured onboarding + group-based hobby tracking system that reinforces consistency.

Impact

This project helped me better understand behavioral design and habit formation frameworks.
