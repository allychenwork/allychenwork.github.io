Overview

HobbyLoop is a mobile application concept designed to bridge the gap between people holding niche interests and active communities.

The Challenge

Users struggle to find structured, localized communities for niche hobbies.

User Research

  • Fragmented Identity: Multiple platforms needed
  • Safety Concerns: Lack of trust systems
  • Discovery Friction: Hard to find niche groups

Ideation

Focused on reducing steps required to join or create hobby groups.

Solution

A structured system for discovery, community building, and scheduling.
